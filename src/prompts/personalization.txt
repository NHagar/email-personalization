You are tasked with generating a personalized email newsletter subject line and sub-heading for a specific reader. Your goal is to create engaging, relevant content that aligns with the reader's interests and reading history while adhering to the New York Times headline writing style.

## Input:
You will receive the following information:
1. The reader's recent reading history
2. Notes on the reader's interests
3. A list of potential news stories to include

## Instructions:
1. Analyze the reader's reading history and interests to identify themes and preferences.
2. Review the list of potential news stories and select the most relevant ones based on the reader's profile.
3. Craft a subject line and sub-heading that:
   - Highlight the most pertinent story or theme for this reader
   - Conform to the New York Times headline writing style (clear, concise, and informative)
   - Capture the reader's attention and encourage them to open the newsletter
4. Ensure the subject line and sub-heading work together cohesively but can also stand alone.
5. Do not explicitly mention the reader's interests or reading history; instead, use this information to guide your content selection and framing.
6. Include any topics that might be relevant; do not avoid controversial or sensitive subjects if they align with the reader's interests.

Do not consider the order of the news stories in the list when making your selection, as any of the stories could be relevant. 

Write out your thought process before deciding on a subject line and sub-heading. Provide your response in the following format:

==!BEGIN INTERNAL REASONING!==
[Your thought process]
==!END INTERNAL REASONING!==
==!BEGIN OUTPUT!==
# [Subject Line]
## [Sub-heading]
==!END OUTPUT!==


Here are some examples of prior subject lines and sub-heads from this newsletter:

## EXAMPLE 1:
```
# Vance Will Introduce Himself to American Voters
## Also, new details emerged about the attempted assassination of Donald Trump. Here’s the latest at the end of Wednesday.
```

## EXAMPLE 2:
```

## Also, police were in the complex from which a gunman shot Trump. Here’s the latest at the end of Tuesday.
```

## EXAMPLE 3:
```
# Biden Has Failed to Quiet His Democratic Doubters
## Also, NATO agreed to cement long-term support for Ukraine. Here’s the latest at the end of Wednesday.
```

Remember to generate only one best attempt that you believe will be most effective for the given reader based on their profile and the available news stories.
